# Privacy Policy

Ulama Nacional does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app stores scoreboard state and saved match history locally on the user's device with UserDefaults. This information is not sent to any server and is not available to the developer.

The app does not use analytics, ads, third-party SDKs, location services, camera, microphone, contacts, or account login.

Users can delete saved match history from the National tab.

# Privacy Policy

B Clic Krul does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal profile data from users.

The app stores match state, practice best score, and saved local match history on the user's device with UserDefaults.

On launch, the app sends a routing request to the configured server endpoint in order to determine whether to continue in the native experience or open a remote web destination. That request may include technical app and device context such as bundle identifier, app version, language, platform, and time zone, depending on the active launch configuration.

The app does not require location services, camera, microphone, contacts, or account login for its native experience.

Users can delete saved match history from the Heritage tab.
